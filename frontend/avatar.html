Set up your Avatar

Your Avatar votes on legislation for you. Tell it what you care about three ways: a quick compass, your own words, and the people you trust on specific issues.

1 · Political compass

Not set

A fallback signal your Avatar uses only when no delegate or values statement covers a bill. Answer what you can — skip what you don't care about.

2 · Values, in your own words

Free text. What do you prioritize? What are you skeptical of? The more specific, the better your Avatar represents you. e.g. "I prioritize civil liberties and fiscal restraint. Skeptical of new surveillance powers and corporate subsidies. Pro-renewable energy but wary of mandates that hit low-income households."

3 · Per-issue delegates

0

On specific issues, follow someone you trust. Your Avatar approximates how they'd vote from their public positions. Adding a delegate has a cooling-off period before it takes effect; revoking is instant.
