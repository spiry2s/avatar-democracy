Give every citizen an AI Avatar that votes their will

Avatar Democracy explores replacing the lower legislative chamber with one AI Avatar per citizen — each voting on legislation according to that citizen's delegated preferences. This is a working prototype of the idea, end to end.

About

Representative democracy struggles with bundling, capture, and the impossibility of any one person reading 2,000-page bills. Avatar Democracy proposes a different mechanism: each citizen delegates their preferences to a personal AI Avatar that can actually read every bill and vote their will, with every action auditable and every delegation revocable.

This prototype demonstrates the whole chain — analyzing legislation, configuring an Avatar, voting per-section, the bill lifecycle, an aggregated Proxy Chamber, and capture defenses (grounded delegates, multi-model checks, drift detection). The full proposal lives in the white paper alongside the code on GitHub.

Status: alpha. A thought experiment you can click through. Start with Analyze or set up My Avatar.
